Le Rouret

Our luxury villa to rent in Le Rouret is well situated to explore the Cote D’azur and Grasse, the perfume capital of the World. Le Rouret is a charming Provencal village typical of the region with its traditional bakers, cheeses, grocery stores, pork butchers, flower shop, chemist and a church. It is the ideal place to discover all the charms of our beautiful region. The perfect place to spend a healthy holiday!

Noted for its great restaurants, including the Michelin starred Le Clos St Pierre and the Bistro du Clos which is owned by the same people and is open for breakfast. A very good Pizzeria, indian restaurant and a supermarket is just a 5 minute drive away

There are also many golf courses in the area.
