Location & Travel

35 mins from the coast with good public transport connections

Villa Vert Bocage is the ideal location for a relaxing holiday experience.

Nearest airports: Nice/Marseille/Toulon/Turin/Avignon/Nimes
Nearest beach: approx 20km
Nearest motorway: A8 Junction 46 at approx 17km
Nearest ferry port: Nice/Cannes/Marseille
Nearest railway station: Grasse approx 5km or Cagnes sur Mer (20 mins away)

Driving from Nice airport

Take A8 from airport to junction 47 and then D2085 in the direction of Grasse to the centre of Le Rouret, turn right at the set of traffic lights which is a cross roads next to the Mairie (town hall), bear left next to dance hall in middle of road, drive approx 200 yards and bear right onto Chemin Des Pierres Du Moulin. Tolls are applicable on the A8.

35 minute travel time by private car or taxi

Bus from Nice Airport

  • Walk to Nice Aéroport Promenade
  • Board the Department Des Alpes Maritimes 500 bus
  • Regular buses to and from Le Rouret

    • 37 minutes travel time to Le Rouret via Line 500 bus
