Le Rouret

Great South of France Location

Our luxury villa to rent in Le Rouret is just a short stroll to the centre of the village. Le Rouret is a pretty Provencal village well located to explore the Cote D’azur and Grasse, the perfume capital of the World.

Not far from the hustle and bustle of the big tourist cities, you will enjoy the charm and calm of the Provencal countryside. However, you will not feel isolated, as well as being close to Nice, Cannes and Antibes, the villiage is also a short distance to charming Valbonne with its beautiful market on Friday mornings, Biot with its famous glassworks, the perched village of Gourdon with its magnificent panoramic views, not forgetting the medieval church and the monastery, Saint-Paul-de-Vence which is the city of artists, the Gorges du Loup, the many hiking trails, golf courses, equestrian centers … and all the leisure and water sports on the sea.

You could also spend a day in the Lérins Islands by boat shuttle from Cannes, or why not spend a day in Italy with the large market of San Remo on Tuesdays and Saturdays ?

Le Rouret is on the route Napoleon and is less than an hour away from the stunning Gorge du Verdon canyon

We are also close to the mountains of the Southern Alps where various sites offer activities such as canyoning, tree climbing (one of the largest zip lines in Europe is at La Colmiane) and climbing.
