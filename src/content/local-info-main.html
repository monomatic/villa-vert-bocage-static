Places to visit near our Holiday Villa Vert Bocage

Staying in our Holiday Villa Vert Bocage you will not be short of things to do and places to visit, Here are some of our top recommendations for day trips, afternoon drives or relaxing in the sunshine.

On the coast – the famous beaches of Cote D’ Azure are a short drive away where you can find diving, surfing, swimming, tennis, walking, sailing, cycling, fishing, horse riding, wind-surfing, water skiing,  paragliding, and cart racing.

Walking ( Camp Romain) – From the Villa there is a beautiful walk up to the top of the hill which gives a beautiful panoramic view of the surroundings including views down to the coast. The area used to have a Roman encampment of which the remains are still visible today,

Free fitness equipment in the park hillside – For people who want to keep their fitness up there are also the free to use gym machines on the walk up the hill, the machines are in the open air and are the same type which are now being installed in UK municipal parks.

Golf – Opio-Valbonne Golf Club is very nearby and offers a good test and provides a range of facilities including a restaurant, swimming pool and tennis courts.

Lake – Lac St Cassien is only 21km away and is a jewel of the eastern Var, this lake offers many water sports (swimming, rowing, pedal boats, canoes, rowing boats, fishing…) as well as bird watching.

River walk and waterfalls – Loup River which is 15km away offers a river walk and waterfall to cool down in!

Gourdon - Breathtaking Views

Listed as one of the most beautiful villages in France, the Château and surrounding medieval buildings of Gourdon are not to be missed. The ancient stone built shops stock an interesting variety of local hand made products from soap and perfume to glassware and silk.

The panoramic views are exceptional as is the drive through the surrounding countryside.

This beautiful village is well worth the 2 hour (6.5 mile) walk or 15 minute drive from Villa Vert Bocage.
